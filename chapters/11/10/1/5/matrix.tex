The mid point of $PB$ is
\begin{align}
\vec{M} =\frac{1}{2}(\vec{P}+\vec{B})
	= \myvec{4 \\ -2}  
\end{align}
which is equal to the direction vector of $OM$.
\begin{align}
\because \vec{M} \equiv
	 \myvec{1 \\ -\frac{1}{2}},
	m = -\frac{1}{2}
\end{align}
which is the desired slope.
